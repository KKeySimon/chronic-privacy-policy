Privacy Policy

Cronic — Last updated: May 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Cronic ("the app") handles your information. Your privacy is important to us and we have designed the app to collect as little data as possible.

Information We Collect

Cronic only stores data that you enter yourself:

  • Time entries you log (title, category, duration, type)
  • App settings and preferences

We do not collect your name, email address, location, or any other personal information.

How Your Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally on your device. If you have iCloud enabled, your data may be synced across your personal Apple devices via Apple's iCloud service. This sync is managed entirely by Apple and is subject to Apple's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

Cronic offers optional in-app purchases through Apple's App Store. All payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not receive or store your payment information.

Third-Party Services

Cronic does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. We do not share your data with any third parties.

Children's Privacy

Cronic is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
